Navigation
Zoom in/out   - Ctrl, scroll wheel on mouse
Zoom to fit in page - space bar
scroll with mouse - space bar, left click and drag



Reorder inputs/outputs in simulink block
1. Double click block to enter block
2. Double click the input/output port that you want to reorder
3. Change Port Number to desired number (this is what determines the order seen in the simulink block)
